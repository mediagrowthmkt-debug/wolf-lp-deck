Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about deck renovation

How long does a deck renovation typically take?

+

Most deck renovations take 1-3 weeks from demo to completion. A straightforward board replacement might be done in a week, while a full custom build with pergola, lighting, and multi-level design may take closer to 3 weeks. We provide a clear timeline upfront so there are no surprises.

Should I go with wood or composite decking?

+

It depends on your priorities. Wood (like pressure-treated or cedar) costs less upfront and has a natural look, but needs regular staining and sealing. Composite (like Trex or TimberTech) costs more initially but is virtually maintenance-free and lasts 25-50 years. We'll help you choose based on your budget, lifestyle, and goals.

Will a deck renovation increase my property value?

+

Yes! A quality deck renovation typically returns 65-80% of your investment at resale, and it dramatically increases buyer appeal. In Massachusetts, outdoor living space is a premium feature that buyers actively seek. And you get to enjoy it every single day until then.

Do you handle permits for deck projects?

+

Absolutely. Most deck projects in Massachusetts require a building permit, especially if you're changing the footprint or height. We handle the entire permit process applications, plans, inspections so you don't have to deal with any of it.

Can you add features like a pergola, fire pit area, or built-in seating?

+

Yes that's what makes a Wolf deck special. We design and build custom features like pergolas, integrated lighting, fire pit areas, built-in benches, planters, and outdoor kitchens. We design around how you actually use your outdoor space, not just a basic rectangle.
